## 4. Risk Management

| Risk | Probability | Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| LLM API instability | Medium | High | Multi-model hot-switching, local backup model |
| Agent accuracy below target | Medium | High | Continuous evaluation, legal consultant feedback loop |
| Competitor early release | High | Medium | Accelerate MVP, capture market first |
| Legal compliance risk | Low | High | Legal consultant involvement throughout, mandatory disclaimer |
| Team member departure | Low | Medium | Complete documentation, distributed knowledge, no single point of dependency |
