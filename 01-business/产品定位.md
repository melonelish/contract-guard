> Version: v1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-06-15

---

## I. One-Line Positioning

**"Understand a contract's risks in 3 minutes"** — a one-click contract risk scanning tool for China's 40 million SMEs.

---

## II. Brand Story

China has 40 million SMEs, 90% without legal staff. Every contract signed is a gamble — you don't know if the liquidated damages clause on page 7 is legally valid, if the jurisdiction clause on page 12 is a trap, or if the contract is missing a confidentiality clause.

What ContractGuard does is simple: you toss in a contract, it marks every risk, tells you which clause is illegal, which is unfair, and what it should be changed to. It's not replacing lawyers — it lets you know where the problems are before you even see a lawyer.

| Value Dimension | Description |

## IV. User Mental Model

### Before Use

> "Is there anything wrong with this contract? I can't understand it, but once I sign I'm responsible. Should I spend ¥500 to have a lawyer look at it? But how do I know if they're reliable..."

### After Use

> "Upload → wait 15 minutes → see 3 red risks, 5 yellow warnings. Liquidated damages are too high, modification suggests capping at 20%. I sent the report directly to the other party, revised and signed. Peace of mind."

---

## VI. Product Boundaries (What It Is Not)

| Boundary | Description |
|---|---|
| **Not a Lawyer** | Does not provide legal opinions, does not draft contracts, does not appear in court |
| **Not an E-Signature** | Does not replace Fadada/Shangshangqian's e-signature function |
| **Does Not Judge the Other Party** | Only analyzes risks, does not judge "is the other party a fraud" |
| **Non-Contracts Not Reviewed** | Does not review indictments, judgments, or administrative documents |
| **Does Not Guarantee 100% Accuracy** | Positioned as an assistive tool, important contracts still require lawyer confirmation |

## VIII. Brand Tone

| Dimension | Style |
|---|---|
| **Rational** | Every conclusion is supported by statutes/precedents, no unfounded judgments |
