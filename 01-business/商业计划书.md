# Business Plan

> Version: v1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-06-15 | Classification: Confidential

---

## I. Executive Summary

ContractGuard is an intelligent contract risk review system based on LLM multi-agent collaboration, targeting China's 40 million SMEs and an annual demand of over 20 million commercial contract reviews. In the current market, 90% of SMEs have no legal team, and contract review relies entirely on the personal experience of founders/business managers, with an average of 3-5 risks missed per contract. ContractGuard enters the market with a dual model of SaaS + private deployment, aiming to serve 5,000 enterprise customers and exceed ¥30 million ARR within 18 months.

---

## II. Market Pain Points

### 2.1 Current State of Contract Review

| Enterprise Type | Current State | Pain Point |
|---|---|---|
| Micro/Small Enterprise (<50 people) | Owner reviews contracts personally | Doesn't understand legal terminology, relies entirely on experience |
| Medium Enterprise (50-500 people) | Has 1-2 legal staff | High contract volume, legal staff overwhelmed |
| Large Enterprise (>500 people) | Has a legal team | Inconsistent review standards, slow onboarding for new hires |
| Law Firm | Lawyers conduct manual review | Repetitive work accounts for 60%, thin margins |

### 2.2 Quantitative Data

- China adds **50 million+** enterprise contracts annually (SAIC data)
- Average legal spending for SMEs **< ¥50,000/year**
- Manual review of a 30-page contract takes **~3 hours** on average
- Human review miss rate (missed risks) approximately **25-30%**
- Contract disputes account for **40%** of civil cases

## III. Solution

### 3.1 Product Positioning

"The first line of defense for enterprise legal affairs" — not legal consulting, only contract risk scanning. Upload a contract and receive a review report with statute citations, precedent references, and modification suggestions.

### 3.2 Core Differentiation

| Comparison Dimension | Traditional Legal | Competitors (Fadada etc.) | ContractGuard |
|---|---|---|---|
| Review Method | Manual clause-by-clause review | Keyword template matching | LLM semantic understanding + multi-step reasoning |
| Statute Citation | From memory | None | Auto-retrieval + original text citation |
| Precedent Reference | Requires additional search | None | RAG retrieval of similar precedents |
| Clause Contradiction Detection | Present but easy to miss | None | Automatic Agent cross-validation |
| Review Report | Handwritten or verbal | Simple annotations | Structured report + modification suggestions |
| Time per Contract | 3 hours | 5 minutes | 15 minutes |
| Cost per Contract | ~¥300 | ~¥10 | ~¥3 |

## VII. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| LLM hallucination causing review errors | Medium | High | Seven-layer hallucination control plan (see 03-agent documentation) |
| Legal compliance risk (no license) | Low | High | Positioned as "assistive tool" not "legal opinion", mandatory disclaimer |
| Big tech entry (Fadada/Alibaba Cloud) | High | Medium | Differentiate on AI capability not template matching, maintain tech leadership |
| Data security compliance | Low | Medium | SOC2 certification + Classified Protection Level 3 |
